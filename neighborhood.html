About Lehi

One of the city's main attractions is Thanksgiving Point, a large complex that includes a museum, gardens, and a golf course. The Museum of Ancient Life at Thanksgiving Point is home to one of the world's largest displays of dinosaur skeletons, including the famous Utahraptor. The gardens at Thanksgiving Point are also a popular destination, with over 55 acres of themed gardens, including a butterfly biosphere and a working farm.

Lehi is also home to several high-tech companies, including Adobe, and Microsoft. These companies have helped to fuel the city's rapid growth and have attracted a highly educated workforce to the area.
